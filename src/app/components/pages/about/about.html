A Story Born From
Our Streets

In the lanes of Srilanga, there are talented people with big dreams — a grandmother making the best homemade pickle, a young mother weaving beautiful crafts, a local farmer with the freshest produce.

But they had no easy way to reach customers. No platform, no support, no voice.

Potikada was created to change that. We believe that the smallest seller deserves the same opportunity as any big brand. When you shop here, you're not just buying a product — you're changing someone's life.
