Order Processing Time

Once your order is placed and payment is confirmed, we process and hand it over to our courier partner within 1–2 working days. Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays will be processed the next working day.

💡 Since many of our products are handmade by local entrepreneurs, some items may need a little extra preparation time. We will let you know if there is any delay.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your location and the weight of your order. You will see the exact shipping cost before you confirm your purchase — no hidden charges.

🎁 We may offer free shipping promotions from time to time. Keep an eye on our website for special offers!
💳

Payment Options

We offer two convenient ways to pay for your order:

🌐

Online Payment

Pay securely via PayHere using your debit card, credit card, or internet banking. Your payment is processed safely and your order is confirmed instantly.

💵

Cash on Delivery (COD)

Prefer to pay when your order arrives? No problem! Pay cash directly to the courier when your package is delivered to your door.

⚠️ For COD orders, please have the exact amount ready for the courier.
🔍

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, we will send you a tracking number via WhatsApp or email so you can follow your package every step of the way.

You can also contact us anytime at 076 193 3305 or info@potikada.lk to check on your order status.

🛡️

Damaged or Lost Parcels

We pack every order with care. However, if your item arrives damaged or your parcel is lost in transit, please contact us within 48 hours of the expected delivery date.

We will work with the courier to investigate and resolve your issue as quickly as possible — either with a replacement or a full refund.

📸 Please take photos of any damaged packaging or items when you receive them. This helps us resolve your case faster.
📬

Incorrect Delivery Address

Please double-check your delivery address before placing your order. If you realise you have entered the wrong address, contact us immediately via WhatsApp at 076 193 3305.

We can update the address if the order has not yet been dispatched. Once an order is with the courier, we may not be able to make changes and any re-delivery costs will be the customer's responsibility.
