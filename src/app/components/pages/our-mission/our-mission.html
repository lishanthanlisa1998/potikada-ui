📍 Born in Hatton

A Small Town Idea
With a Big Heart

Potikada started because we looked around Hatton and saw so much talent going unnoticed. Homemade foods, handmade crafts, fresh garden produce — all hidden from the rest of Sri Lanka.

We asked ourselves — what if we could change that? What if a grandmother's famous pickle could reach a family in Colombo? What if a young maker in Hatton could build a real income from home?

That's the story of Potikada. And it's still being written — one local seller at a time.
