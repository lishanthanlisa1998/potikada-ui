How to Return an Order

It's simple and straightforward — just 4 easy steps.

01

Contact Us First

WhatsApp us at 076 193 3305 or email info@potikada.lk within 7 days of receiving your order. Tell us your order number and what the problem is.

02

We Review Your Request

Our team will review your request within 1–2 working days and let you know if your return is approved. We may ask for photos of the item.

03

Send the Item Back

Once approved, send the item back in its original condition and packaging. We will give you the return address. Please use a trackable courier service.

04

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 5–7 working days to your original payment method.

✅

Eligible for Return

  • Item received is damaged or broken
  • Wrong item was delivered to you
  • Item is significantly different from the description
  • Item is missing parts or accessories
  • Order was not delivered within the agreed timeframe
❌

Not Eligible for Return

  • Items that have been used or opened (unless defective)
  • Perishable items such as food or fresh produce
  • Personal hygiene products once opened
  • Custom or made-to-order items
  • Items purchased on sale or with a discount (unless defective)
  • Return requests made after 7 days of delivery
💰

How Refunds Are Paid

Refunds are returned using the same method you used to pay:

🌐

Online Payment (PayHere)

Refunded back to your original card or bank account within 5–7 working days after we approve your return.

💵

Cash on Delivery (COD)

Refunded via bank transfer to an account you provide. We will contact you for your bank details once the return is approved.

🏦 Please note your bank may take additional time to process the credit to your account after we issue the refund.
⚖️

Partial Refunds

In some cases, only a partial refund may be granted — for example if an item is returned in a condition different from how it was received, or if only part of an order is being returned.

We will always be transparent and let you know the refund amount before processing it.

🔁

Exchanges

If you received a defective or damaged item, we are happy to exchange it for the same product — subject to availability.

To request an exchange, contact us within 7 days with photos of the damaged item. We will arrange a replacement and cover all shipping costs.
